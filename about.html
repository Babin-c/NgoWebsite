About Silent Voices

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering the deaf community through education, advocacy, and equal opportunities.

Join Our Cause Donate Now

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Silent Voices began as a small community initiative by a group of deaf activists and hearing allies who recognized the systemic barriers faced by the deaf community in our society.

What started as sign language classes in a community center has grown into a nationwide movement with programs in education, employment, accessibility advocacy, and cultural preservation.

Today, we serve over 50,000 individuals annually through our various programs and initiatives, making us one of the leading organizations for deaf empowerment in the country.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

- To create an inclusive society where deaf individuals have equal access to education, employment, and social opportunities through advocacy, awareness, and empowerment programs.

- We strive to break down communication barriers and promote sign language as a fundamental human right for deaf individuals.

Our Vision

- A world where deaf people enjoy the same rights, opportunities, and quality of life as their hearing peers, where sign language is recognized and respected, and where deaf culture is celebrated as part of human diversity.

- We envision a society free from audism and communication barriers.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusion

    We believe in a society where deaf individuals fully participate in all aspects of life without barriers.

  • Equality

    We advocate for equal rights and opportunities for deaf people in education, employment, and social participation.

  • Cultural Pride

    We celebrate deaf culture and sign language as vital parts of human diversity.

  • Empowerment

    We equip deaf individuals with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to advocate for themselves.

  • Collaboration

    We work in partnership with the deaf community, hearing allies, and other organizations to create systemic change.

  • Innovation

    We develop creative solutions to address the unique challenges faced by the deaf community.

Message from Our Director

Sign Language Advocate

"Our mission is to break communication barriers and create a world where sign language connects everyone. Every hand movement tells a story, and we're here to amplify those voices."

Our responsibility goes beyond accessibility. It is about creating respectful, inclusive spaces where sign language users are heard, valued, and empowered.
